Data Privacy Policy

Republic Act 10173 - Data Privacy Act of 2012

1. Collection of Personal Information

Wanam Sa Bukid Restaurant collects and processes your personal information including but not limited to:

  • Full name
  • Contact number
  • Email address
  • Birthday
  • Booking and reservation details
  • Payment information

2. Purpose of Data Collection

Your personal information is collected for the following purposes:

  • Processing of bookings and reservations
  • Customer service and support
  • Payment processing and verification
  • Marketing communications (with your consent)
  • Compliance with legal obligations

3. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. Your data is stored securely and accessed only by authorized personnel.

4. Your Rights

Under the Data Privacy Act of 2012, you have the right to:

  • Be informed about the collection and processing of your personal data
  • Access your personal data
  • Object to the processing of your personal data
  • Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Request erasure or blocking of your personal data
  • Withdraw consent at any time

5. Data Retention

We retain your personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes for which it was collected, or as required by law.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or transfer your personal information to third parties without your consent, except as required by law or for the fulfillment of our services (e.g., payment processors).

7. Contact Us

For questions, concerns, or requests regarding your personal data, please contact:

8. Updates to Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page and will be effective immediately upon posting.
